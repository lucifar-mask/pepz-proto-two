Beauty

Structural skin renewal from below — not surface cover-up.

Beauty-focused peptides work by replacing signals the skin naturally stops producing with age, rather than masking the surface effects of that decline.

GHK-Cu, a copper-binding peptide the body produces abundantly in youth and far less by the thirties, rebuilds the dermal matrix from below — stimulating collagen, elastin, and glycosaminoglycan synthesis to restore thickness and elasticity while accelerating repair of photodamage and supporting denser hair growth at the follicle.

Rather than sitting on top of the skin like a topical treatment, these peptides work systemically and topically to repair the underlying structure — producing results that compound over weeks rather than fading the moment you stop using them.

  • Collagen & elastin synthesis
  • Skin thickness & elasticity
  • Photodamage repair
  • Hair follicle density
