Prescription Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

PEPZ is a technology platform that connects patients with licensed physicians and accredited compounding pharmacies. All peptide products available through our platform require a valid prescription from a licensed healthcare provider.

1. Medical Consultation Required

Before any peptide product can be dispensed, you must complete a telehealth consultation with one of our partnered physicians. The physician will evaluate your medical history, current health status, and treatment goals to determine if peptide therapy is appropriate for you.

2. Prescription Validity

Prescriptions issued through our platform are valid for a maximum of 90 days from the date of issue. Refills require a follow-up consultation to ensure continued medical appropriateness. Prescriptions are non-transferable and can only be filled at our partnered compounding pharmacies.

3. Compounding Pharmacy Partners

We exclusively work with 503A and 503B compounding pharmacies that meet or exceed USP <797> and USP <795> standards. All compounded medications are prepared using pharmaceutical-grade APIs (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients) sourced from FDA-registered suppliers.

4. Patient Eligibility

You must be 18 years of age or older to use our services. Certain medical conditions may preclude you from receiving peptide therapy, including but not limited to active cancer, pregnancy, and certain cardiovascular conditions. The prescribing physician has final discretion on all treatment decisions.

Lab Reports & Quality Assurance

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Quality and purity are foundational to everything we do. Every batch of peptides dispensed through our platform undergoes rigorous third-party testing before reaching patients.

1. Third-Party Testing

All peptide APIs are tested by independent ISO 17025-accredited laboratories. Testing includes HPLC (High-Performance Liquid Chromatography) for purity verification, mass spectrometry for identity confirmation, and endotoxin testing for safety assurance.

2. Certificate of Analysis (COA)

A Certificate of Analysis is available for every batch of medication dispensed. COAs include detailed purity percentages, identity confirmation data, and microbial testing results. Patients may request COA documentation through their account dashboard or by contacting our support team.

3. Storage & Handling

Our pharmacy partners maintain cold-chain storage for temperature-sensitive compounds. Peptides requiring refrigeration are stored at 2–8°C and shipped with validated cold-chain packaging to ensure stability during transit.

4. Batch Tracking

Every vial and package is assigned a unique batch number for complete traceability. In the unlikely event of a quality concern, we can rapidly identify and recall specific batches to ensure patient safety.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

PEPZ Bio-Tech Inc. ("PEPZ," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our services, including your name, contact details, medical history, consultation notes, and prescription information. We also collect technical data such as IP addresses and device information for security purposes.

2. HIPAA Compliance

As a healthcare technology platform, we are fully compliant with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). All protected health information (PHI) is encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Our infrastructure is hosted in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to facilitate healthcare services, including physician consultations, prescription processing, and pharmacy fulfillment. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with our partnered physicians and pharmacies solely for the purpose of providing your care.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. You may also request a copy of your medical records at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy officer at privacy@pepz.com.
