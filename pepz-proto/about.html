About PEPZ

Your body already speaks peptide.

PEPZ exists to make the body's own signaling language accessible, safe, and prescribed. We're not a pharmacy and we're not a supplement brand — we're the layer that connects you to licensed physicians and accredited compounding pharmacies, so peptide therapy happens the way medicine should.

Our Mission

For decades, the peptides that regulate repair, recovery, and aging lived in research papers and specialist clinics — hard to access, harder to verify. We built PEPZ to change the distribution, not the science.

Every product on the platform is pharmaceutical-grade, prescribed by a licensed physician after a real consultation, and dispensed by a compounding pharmacy that meets or exceeds federal standards. No gray-market vials, no guesswork, no "research use only" disclaimers hiding a lack of oversight.

The goal is simple: give people a legitimate, clinical path to the same signaling molecules their bodies already use — with the testing, traceability, and physician judgment that legitimacy requires.

How We Operate

Licensed Physician Network

Every prescription is issued by a licensed physician who reviews your medical history and goals in a telehealth consultation. The prescribing physician holds final discretion on all treatment decisions.

Accredited Compounding Pharmacies

We work exclusively with 503A and 503B compounding pharmacies meeting or exceeding USP <797> and USP <795> standards, using pharmaceutical-grade APIs from FDA-registered suppliers.

Third-Party Tested

Every batch is tested by independent ISO 17025-accredited labs — HPLC for purity, mass spectrometry for identity, endotoxin testing for safety. A Certificate of Analysis is available for every batch dispensed.

HIPAA-Compliant Infrastructure

Protected health information is encrypted in transit and at rest with AES-256, hosted in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers. We never sell your personal information.

You must be 18 or older to use PEPZ. Certain conditions may preclude peptide therapy, and anyone taking medications or managing a health condition is routed to a physician consultation before any recommendation is made.
